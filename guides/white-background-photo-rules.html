White background photo rules

How white or off-white background rules affect visa and green card photos, and when paid background cleanup helps.

Why the background matters

A busy or dark background can make a photo look non-compliant even if the crop size is correct. A plain white or off-white background is commonly expected for official photo submissions.

What goes wrong most often

  • Gray shadows behind the head
  • Patterned walls
  • Furniture or room edges in the frame
  • Hair blending into the background

Why make this a premium feature

Background removal consumes a paid API call. Making it premium protects your margins and creates a clear upsell path from the free crop/export workflow.

Recommended premium message

Show the user a strong preview for free, then offer one-click premium cleanup for a white-background result and an ad-free session.

Reminder

Even with background cleanup, the user still needs to check expression, eyewear glare, head angle, and current official instructions.
