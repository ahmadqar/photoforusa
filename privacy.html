Policy

Privacy Policy

This starter site processes image uploads locally in the browser for cropping. If premium background removal is used, the uploaded image is sent to your configured remove.bg account for that request. Payments are handled by Stripe on Stripe-hosted checkout pages.

What this template stores

This project stores session cookies for human verification and premium access on the browser. These cookies are signed and used to unlock features.

Uploads and premium processing

The core crop workflow runs inside the browser. Premium background removal sends the uploaded image to remove.bg through your Cloudflare Function after payment activation.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. You should add your own merchant name, billing terms, and refund policy before launch.

Analytics and ads

If you add analytics scripts, ad code, affiliate widgets, or remarketing pixels, update this policy to reflect those services and local legal requirements.

Before launch

  • Add your business/legal entity name
  • Add your support email
  • Disclose analytics/ad providers
  • Publish data retention rules

About

PhotoForUSA is a starter product for U.S. visa and green card-style photo preparation, monetization, and Cloudflare deployment.

This website is not affiliated with the U.S. government. Users must review the latest official requirements before submission.
